About

Squadify is a web app created by four college students for the Bitcamp 2021 Hackathon, helping users quickly and easily merge Spotify playlists for group events (or personal use!). Music is a necessity when hanging out with your squad, but creating group playlists can be difficult to organize or maintain, and using a single person's playlist simply isn't as inclusive or enjoyable. That's why we came in with the goal of maximizing squad diversity: we want to choose the songs that the most people can recognize and enjoy while still giving those with more unique tastes their time to shine!

If you have any feedback, including bugs, feature requests, or general notes on the playlist creation, please reach out to us via our feedback form! Thanks for using Squadify!
